About My Blood Donation System

Together, we make life possible — connecting donors, hospitals, and people in need of blood across Myanmar.

Who We Are

My Blood Donation System is a digital platform that simplifies and strengthens the blood donation process across Myanmar. We aim to make blood donation faster, safer, and more transparent for everyone.

Our Mission

To ensure every patient receives timely access to safe and reliable blood donations.

Our Vision

To build a connected network of donors, hospitals, and volunteers across the country.

Our Values

Empathy, transparency, and collaboration — one drop at a time.
